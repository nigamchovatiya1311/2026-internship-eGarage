Vehicle Tips

Important Tips to Keep Your Vehicle Running Freely

Simple habits every car owner should follow & catch problems early and avoid costly breakdowns.

Engine Care
Maintenance Tip · Must-Know

Check Engine Oil Level Every 1,000 km

Low engine oil is the #1 cause of engine damage. Pull out the dipstick every 1,000 km — oil should be between the MIN and MAX marks and appear amber, not black. Dark, gritty oil means it's overdue for a change.

Book an Oil Change
Tyres
Monthly Check

Tyre Pressure — Check Every Month

Under-inflated tyres reduce fuel efficiency by up to 3%, wear unevenly, and can blow out at high speed. Check pressure monthly when cold and keep it at the manufacturer's recommended PSI.

Book Wheel Check
Warning Signs
Act Fast

Never Ignore the Check Engine Light

A glowing check engine light signals a live fault in your vehicle's system. Even if the car seems fine, ignoring it can turn a ₹500 fix into a ₹50,000 repair. Get a diagnostic scan within 48 hours.

Book Diagnostics
Brakes
Safety First

Squeaking Brakes? Don't Wait

A squealing or grinding noise when braking means your brake pads are worn. Driving on bare metal destroys your rotors and risks brake failure. Replace pads immediately when you hear that sound.

Book Brake Service
AC Health
Seasonal Tip

Service Your AC Before Summer

Weak cooling usually means low refrigerant or a clogged cabin filter. Service your AC before April — waiting until peak summer means longer queues, higher heat, and more strain on the compressor.

Book AC Service
